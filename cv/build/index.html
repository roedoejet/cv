Aidan Pine

Linguist & Developer
Hello!

Thanks for visiting my site, which I developed to bring together the various projects that I am involved in. My name is Aidan and I'm a settler of European ancestry born in Victoria, B.C. I'm interested in linguistically and computationally informed language revitalization projects.

I am a full-time application development specialist working on the Indigenous Language Technology project of the National Research Council, the founder and lead developer of Mother Tongues, and part-time technology consultant for the First Nations & Endangered Languages Program and Linguistics Department at the University of British Columbia.
